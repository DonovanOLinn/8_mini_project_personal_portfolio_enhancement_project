Selected Tabletop Games

I have designed and developed many tabletop games. Below you will find the pitch videos for 4 of my designs. Each design is mechanically very different from the last, having been designed for different target audiences. The connective tissue lies in my my three-pronged design philosophy:

  1. 1.

    Create a wholesome core (for instance, in Witchcrafters every decision a player makes benefits their opponents)

  2. 2.

    Structure the game to ensure highly interactive scenarios (allowing the players to embrace the narrative of the game and stay continuously engaged)

  3. 3.

    Center the gameplay around making meaningful decisions (activating critical-thinking skills in players)
